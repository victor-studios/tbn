Keiko Nakahara

Keiko Nakahara, a Japanese director of photography/cinematographer, has etched her name into the annals of cinematic history with her unparalleled skill and visionary approach to storytelling through the lens. She is best known for her groundbreaking work on the CGI epic tale, "Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior." The film, which grossed over US$54 million worldwide in 2023, was not only a major success at the box office but also marked its place as the highest-grossing Hindi film of 2020, as well as the highest-grossing Indian film of that year. Nakahara's collaboration with renowned talents such as Ryan Gosling and Priyanka Chopra Jonas speaks volumes of her prowess and the respect she commands within the industry. Her resume is a testament to her impressive career, showcasing a range of projects that highlight her versatility and dedication to the art of cinematography. Her work is characterized by its elegance, epic scale, and the depth of true craftsmanship. Nakahara's ability to weave visual narratives that are both captivating and emotionally resonant has made her a sought-after talent in the film industry. Through her lens, she brings to life stories that transcend cultural and geographical boundaries, making her contributions to cinema both invaluable and unforgettable.
